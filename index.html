M-Pesa Withdrawal Charges in 2026

As of March 2026, M-Pesa withdrawal charges remain consistent with the updated tariffs established in late 2025. Safaricom continues to operate under a tiered structure where withdrawing from an agent is slightly more expensive than sending money, but significantly cheaper than withdrawing via an ATM.

Below are the current withdrawal rates for your reference.

Key Transaction Limits (2026)

  • Maximum Daily Limit: You can transact up to Ksh 500,000 per day.
  • Maximum Account Balance: Your M-Pesa wallet can hold up to Ksh 500,000.
  • Maximum Per Transaction: The limit for a single withdrawal or transfer is Ksh 250,000.
  • Minimum Withdrawal: You cannot withdraw less than Ksh 50 at an agent.

How to Check M-Pesa Withdrawal Charges

You can use the M-Pesa Withdrawal Charges Calculator to get instant, accurate fee breakdowns for both Agent and ATM transactions.

Note: To withdraw from an agent, you must have your Original ID (National ID or Passport).
